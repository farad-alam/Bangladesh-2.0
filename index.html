Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh fosters innovation through digital transformation, encouraging startups, and investing in research. Special focus is placed on expanding IT infrastructure, promoting e-governance, and creating tech hubs. These initiatives aim to position the country as a global leader in technology-driven solutions.

Sustainability

Bangladesh prioritizes sustainability by promoting renewable energy, afforestation, and climate-resilient farming. Focused on reducing emissions and managing resources efficiently, the country is also investing in waste recycling and green technologies.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh is improving infrastructure through major projects like the Padma Bridge, enhancing transportation, trade, and connectivity. Urban planning focuses on smart cities, sustainable housing, and efficient waste management.

Empowerment

Bangladesh focuses on inclusive growth by enhancing education, vocational training, and financial inclusion. Empowering women and marginalized communities, promoting digital literacy, and expanding social safety nets are key priorities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Our strength lies in our people—their resilience, innovation, and dreams. Together, we will build a nation rooted in empowerment, sustainability, infrastructure, and innovation. Let us empower every individual, ensuring no one is left behind. Let us embrace sustainability, preserving our environment for generations. Let us construct world-class infrastructure to connect every corner of our land. Let us inspire innovation to lead in the global arena."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
